Normal Skin Type
[nor·​mal/skin/tīp] noun

Skin is over all well balanced that is not overally dry or oily. The main cause of this skin type is from genetics.





Skin Care Routine for Normal Skin Type



1.

Exfoliate
 Exfoliate 1~3 times a week to help remove dead skin cells and pore clogging.
*Try to avoid grainy scrubs and stick with chemical exfoliants. (ingredients like retnol, AHA/BHA)*




Cleansing
 Cleanse twice a day, in the morning and at night to remove dirt and excess oil. Use a gentle non-foaming cleanser.
*Make sure it does not strip all of your oil because it will create more oil*

3.

Serum
 Apply after washing your face. You can use products catered to what your skin needs!
*Anti-aging (retinol,bakuchiol), Skin brightening (vitamin C, glycolic acid, kojic acid) Hydrating (hyaluronic acid, vitamin B5) Acne-fighting (salicylic acid, niacinamide)*

Moisturizer
 Apply a light and hydrating moisturizer twice a day to lock in your serum and hydrate your skin.
*You can use lotion (less oil) or cream (more oil), based on how dry your skin is*

5.

Sunscreen
 Apply sunscreen with a SPF30 or higher in the morning every single day, regardless of whether you stay indoors or outdoors!
*Re-apply every 2~3 hours*
