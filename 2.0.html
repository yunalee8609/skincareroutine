Oily Skin Type
[ˈoilē/skin/tīp] noun

Skin has lots of excess sebum with a shiny complexion. The main cause of this skin type is from genetics.





Skin Care Routine for Oily Skin Type



1.

Exfoliate
 Exfoliate 1~3 times a week to help remove dead skin cells and pore clogging.
*Try to avoid grainy scrubs and stick with chemical exfoliants. (ingredients like retnol, AHA/BHA)*




Cleansing
 Cleanse twice a day, in the morning and at night to remove dirt and excess oil. Use a foaming gentle cleanser in the morning and an exfolating cleanser in the evening
*Make sure to also cleanse after a workout or heavy sweating*

3.

Serum
 Apply after washing your face. You can use products catered to what your skin needs!
*Anti-aging (retinol,bakuchiol), Skin brightening (vitamin C, glycolic acid, kojic acid) Hydrating (hyaluronic acid, vitamin B5) Acne-fighting (salicylic acid, niacinamide)*

Moisturizer
 Apply a light weight, oil free, water-based moisturizer twice a day to lock in your serum and hydrate your skin.
*You can use a heavier product in the evening if you feel that it is a little dry, but avoid using thick oil directly on the face*

5.

Sunscreen
 Apply a light weight sunscreen with a SPF30 or higher in the morning every single day, regardless of whether you stay indoors or outdoors! (ingredients with titanium dioxide, zinc oxide)
*Re-apply every 2~3 hours*

Face Mask
 Apply a clay face mask 1~3 times a week, the clay obsorbs the excess sebum, so it helps with clogged pores. Make sure to wash the mask off thoroughly and cleanse after using a clay mask
*You can also use a blotting paper regularly to control the sebum*
