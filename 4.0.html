Combination Skin Type
[com·​bi·​na·​tion/skin/tīp] noun

Skin is oily in some place and dry in other spots. The cause can be from genetics, horomonal issues, or
environmental factors.





Skin Care Routine for Combination Skin Type



1.

Exfoliate
 Exfoliate 1~3 times a week to help remove dead skin cells and pore clogging.
*Try to avoid grainy scrubs and stick with chemical exfoliants. (ingredients like retnol, AHA/BHA)*




Cleansing
 Cleanse with a gentle cleanser twice a day, in the morning and at night to remove dirt and excess oil. Use a gentle non-foaming cleanser.
*Make sure it does not strip all of your oil because it will create more oil*

3.

Serum
 Apply after washing your face. You can use products catered to what your skin needs!
*Anti-aging (retinol,bakuchiol), Skin brightening (vitamin C, glycolic acid, kojic acid) Hydrating (hyaluronic acid, vitamin B5) Acne-fighting (salicylic acid, niacinamide)*

Moisturizer
 Apply a light oil-free moisturizer in oily areas, and heavier cream in the dry areas
*You can use lotion (less oil) or cream (more oil), based on where and how dry your skin is*

4.
5.

Sunscreen
 Apply sunscreen with a SPF30 or higher in the morning every single day, regardless of whether you stay indoors or outdoors!
*Re-apply every 2~3 hours*

Face Mask
 Use a face mask 1~3 times a week to balance the overall skin. You can use a clay mask in areas that are oily, and a gel or cream based mask in the dry areas.

6.

Pro Tip! Use a blotting paper to control the oily parts of the face. Also try to use a lightweight makeup, so your pores do not get clogged *Cleansing is key if you wear make up!!*
